About MealsApp

MealsApp is your gateway to global flavors and easy recipes. Discover thousands of meals from around the world, search by name, filter by category, and save your favorites. Powered by TheMealDB API, our app makes meal planning and cooking fun, simple, and inspiring for everyone!

  • Browse meals by category
  • Search for specific recipes
  • View detailed meal information
  • Save your favorite meals
  • Enjoy a beautiful, easy-to-use interface
